Why Remit

Flexible work is becoming a legal right — and employers have no system built for it.

Victoria is legislating a statutory right to work from home; the right to disconnect and a positive psychosocial-safety duty already apply nationally. Every WFH decision is now a documented legal event, yet most are managed in email threads and spreadsheets. Surveillance "bossware" answers the wrong question, damages trust, and is increasingly unlawful. Remit is the opposite: it records the employer's decisions and duties — so they're consistent, defensible and discharged — instead of watching the employee.

WFH Governance

A request-to-record engine that makes every flexible-work decision consistent and defensible.
  • Structured request intake & role-suitability assessment — reasoning captured before the decision.
  • Consistency check — flags adverse-action and discrimination risk against prior decisions before a refusal ships.
  • Controlled-vocabulary refusal grounds — no free-text legal exposure.
  • Counsel-approved decision letters, auto-assembled per jurisdiction.
  • Jurisdiction engine — EO Act (Vic) vs Fair Work pathways, parameterised across states.
  • Timestamped audit trail — a 7-year, exportable dispute file.

Wellbeing & WHS

Discharge the psychosocial and ergonomic duty for distributed staff — and prove it.
  • Hazard register with full duty-discharge records per hazard.
  • Hierarchy-of-controls workflow — higher-order first; training never the sole control (VIC rule).
  • Aggregate, consent-based signals — after-hours index & pulse at team level, never individual surveillance.
  • HSR / worker consultation recorded against each hazard.
  • Scheduled & triggered control reviews with a control log.
  • Right-to-disconnect adherence monitoring.

Outcome Contracts

The legitimate answer to "is the work getting done?" — output as the proxy, not presence.
  • Co-set deliverable contracts, visible to both sides.
  • Delivery signals from tools already in use (Jira / CRM / Asana / tickets).
  • Lawful-performance framing — defensible, procedurally fair records.
  • Cycle reviews with sign-off and retention.
  • Privacy by design — APP-grade, zero activity capture, no third-party sharing.
